How Does It Work?

Participants will be provided with a series of jumbled words related to various technical concepts, programming languages, hardware components, and more. Your task is to decipher the words and rearrange the letters to form the correct technical terms. Each correct answer will earn you points, and the participant with the highest score at the end of the round will be crowned the JUMBATRON Champion!

Why Participate?

  1. Test Your Technical Vocabulary: Challenge yourself and expand your knowledge of technical terms.
  2. Sharpen Your Problem-Solving Skills: Enhance your ability to think on your feet and solve puzzles under pressure.
  3. Compete for Prizes: Exciting rewards await the top performers, including exclusive tech gadgets, vouchers, and more.
  4. Fun and Excitement: Experience an exhilarating and entertaining event that combines learning with entertainment.
