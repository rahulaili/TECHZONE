JUMBATRON

Are you ready to put your technical knowledge and problem-solving skills to the test?Join us for an exhilarating round of JUMBATRON, where you'll be faced with a series of jumbled words related to the world of technology. What is JUMBATRON?

JUMBATRON is a First Round that brings together technology enthusiasts, programmers, and anyone passionate about the intricacies of the digital world. It combines the thrill of word puzzles with the challenge of technical terminology. This event aims to test your knowledge, creativity, and quick thinking, as you race against the clock to unravel the jumbled words.

ERROR DEBUGGING is a 2nd Round designed to challenge participants' ability to identify and rectify coding errors.
